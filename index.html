Artist Profile

YOASOBI is a Japanese superduo composed of composer Ayase and singer-songwriter Ikura. Formed in 2019, the duo has since created 22 singles and 6 extended plays.


With a mix of rock, pop and electronic music, their songs relfect modern themes with darker lyrics than the average pop song, allowing them to stand out from the rest.


They rose to fame in a flash with their viral songs, winning awards, topping charts, and creating opening themes for anime.


Their most popular song is "Idol", the opening theme to the anime "OSHI NO KO", was released in April of 2023 and has since surpassed 400 million cumulative streaming plays. It topped the Oricon Combined Singles Chart and Billboard Japan Hot 100 for 21 consecutive weeks, making it the longest-running number-one song in the chart history.
